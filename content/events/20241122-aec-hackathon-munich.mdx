Join us for the AEC Hackathon in Munich, a thrilling event where the brightest minds in Architecture, Engineering, and Construction come together to innovate and collaborate. From November 22-24, 2024, experience a weekend filled with creative problem-solving, hands-on workshops, and opportunities to network with top professionals and enthusiasts from around the globe.

Our last hackathon in Zurich was a huge success with over 250 attendees, and we are excited to bring the same energy and innovation to Munich. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a passionate student, this hackathon is your chance to shape the future of the AEC industry.

## Organiser

This event is proudly organised by TUM Venture Labs and Opensource.construction, in collaboration with industry leaders and experts committed to driving innovation in the AEC sector.
