![AEC Hackathon - Munich Edition](/images/events/20241122_aec_hackathon_munich.jpg "AEC Hackathon - Munich Edition")

Join us for the AEC Hackathon in Munich, a thrilling event where the brightest minds in Architecture, Engineering, and Construction come together to innovate and collaborate. From November 22-24, 2024, experience a weekend filled with creative problem-solving, hands-on workshops, and opportunities to network with top professionals and enthusiasts from around the globe.

Our last hackathon in Zurich was a huge success with over 250 attendees, and we are excited to bring the same energy and innovation to Munich. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a passionate student, this hackathon is your chance to shape the future of the AEC industry.

## Organiser

This event is proudly organised by TUM Venture Labs and Opensource.construction, in collaboration with industry leaders and experts committed to driving innovation in the AEC sector.

## Event Agenda

### Day 1: Friday, February 02

- 13:00         Doors open
- 13:30 – 14:00 Welcome from the organisers: The potential of code-based collaboration for the Swiss Wood Industry
- 14:00 – 15:15 Insights into the practice of a digital Wood industry 
  - Vertically integrated strategies for construction (J. Wiesinger, Gropyus)
  - Possibilities for local collaboration in a fragmented industrial landscape (E. Augustynowicz BFH & Industry Partner tbd.)
  - Obstacles and opportunities for software provider and machinery manufactures (Armin Jud, Cadwork & Patric Jaksic, Technowood)
Gropyus, BFH, Cadwork, Technowood)

- 15:15 - 15:30 Coffee Break

- 15:30 - 16:30 Break-out sessions > learn hands-on with the experts
  - LCA and data availability (L. Trümpler, LT+ / H. Schmid, lignum)
  - Knowledge Bases: Materialarchiv, Holzbaukultur.ch, Prix Lignum Archiv (ZHAW)
  - Prefab Construction Supply Chain (Y. Moradi, MOD)
  - What’s the missing part for enabling automated planning processes in timber buildings? (J. Wiesinger, gropyus)
  - Open Source as an enabler to secure the locational advantages of the Swiss Timber Industry (M. Vomhof, opensource.construction)

16:30 - 16:45 	Coffee Break

16:45 - 17:45 	Presentation & Discussion Break-out session

18:00 – 21:00: Team Formation and Brainstorming Sessions

### Day 2: Saturday, February 03

- 09:00 – 12:00: Hacking
- 12:00 – 13:00: Lunch Break
- 13:00 – 18:00: Hacking and Tech-Talks
- 18:00 – 20:00: Dinner and Networking

### Day 3: Saturday, February 04

- 12:00 – 13:00: Lunch Break
